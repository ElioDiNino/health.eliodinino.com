Deep Breathing

I encourage you to use the transitioning background as a guide for your deep breathing. I recommend breathing in for 4 seconds, holding for 4 seconds, and then breathing out for another 7 seconds. Blue transitioning to pink indicates inhaling, stationary pink is for holding, and pink transitioning back to blue is for exhaling.

To start your breathing press the play/pause button!
